## 👨‍💻 About Me

As an enthusiastic Senior Software Engineer with over five years of practical experience, I specialize in engineering reliable, high-performance, and scalable solutions. I am deeply committed to clean code principles (SOLID), robust software architecture, and the full Software Development Life Cycle. 

Throughout my career, I have delivered over 30 projects across a variety of complex industries, including blockchain (MEV Bots, NFT Marketplaces), cybersecurity simulations, MedTech, AI integration, and e-commerce.

- 🏗️ **Core Focus:** High-performance backend development, database optimization, and scalable architectures.
- 💻 **Tech Stack:** Heavily focused on the JavaScript/TypeScript ecosystem (Node.js, NestJS) alongside robust data management.
- 🌱 **Always Learning:** Constantly exploring modern AI developer tools and advanced system designs.
